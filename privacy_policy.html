Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

At LogiTrack, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and medical-related data provided during registration or order placement.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our site, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Sensitive Data: Health information, prescriptions, and order details, which are handled with extra care under HIPAA compliance.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process orders and manage inventory.
  • Provide customer support and personalized services.
  • Improve our platform and ensure compliance with healthcare regulations.
  • Send updates and notifications (with your consent).
3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers for logistics and payment processing.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.
  • Healthcare partners with your explicit consent.
4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information. Contact us at info@logitrack.com to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage preferences in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.
